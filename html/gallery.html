Why do I like to use polymer clay?

When I was a child I used to live in my mom's clinic, the area was conjusted, so I couldn't go out that much as a kid, my family was also protective but I'm glad they did because I like they way I have become. Since I couldn't go out and my sister was 14 years older than me, I didn't have much things to do, so I used to play with clay, drawing and plants in my backyard.

I developed very good skills for manual work, although I have never study properly how to do it profesionally. Maybe I should do it and and earn some extra money with it. Coming to Canada made me realize I will an extra way of income if I want to keep snowbowling my progression.
